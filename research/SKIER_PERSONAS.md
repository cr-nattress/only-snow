# Skier Personas & Customer Journeys

**Purpose**: Detailed predictive profiles of the top 5 skier segments to inform feature development, data prioritization, and UX design.

---

## 1. The Powder Hunter ("The Stoke Chaser")
**Archetype**: Expert, Obsessive, Spontaneous
**Motto**: *"No Friends on a Powder Day"*

### Profile
*   **Demographics**: 25-45, Skews Male, Local or remote worker with flexible schedule.
*   **Skill Level**: Expert (Double Black/Backcountry).
*   **Gear**: Owns multiple pairs of skis (rock skis, powder skis), season pass holder (Ikon/Epic).

### Predictive Needs (The Journey)
| Phase | Information Need | Predictable Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dreaming** | **Long-range weather models** (10-day out). Identifying storms. | Checks OpenSnow/NOAA 5x/day. Watches freezing levels and wind direction. |
| **Planning** | **Real-time accumulation**. Road closures/accessibility. | Decides destination <24h before trip based on "snow totals" vs "crowd predictions". |
| **Booking** | **Last-minute lodging** (cheap/functional) or sleeping in van/car. | Minimal spend on food/lodging. Maximum spend on gear/access. |
| **The Day** | **Lift Status & Terrain Openings**. Which bowl opens first? | Arrives 1 hour before first chair. Skips lunch to maximize vert. |
| **Sharing** | **Strava stats, GoPro clips**. | Posts deep powder turns to Instagram/TikTok immediately. |

### Critical Data Points for OpenClaw
*   Real-time 24h/48h snow totals (accurate to the inch).
*   Wind hold status (will the upper mountain open?).
*   Backcountry gate status.
*   Webcams (to verify reporting).

## 2. The Chief Family Officer ("The Planner")
**Archetype**: Organized, Stressed, Logistics-Focused
**Motto**: *"Is everyone warm? Do we have the tickets?"*

### Profile
*   **Demographics**: 35-55, Parent, High Household Income.
*   **Skill Level**: Mixed (Parent often intermediate/advanced, kids varying).
*   **Gear**: Renting for kids (growing), owning for adults. High friction in gear management.

### Predictive Needs (The Journey)
| Phase | Information Need | Predictable Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dreaming** | **Resort amenities**, "Kids Ski Free" deals, Ski School reputation. | Plans 3-6 months in advance. Needs reassurance on "family friendliness". |
| **Planning** | **Ski School availability**, Condo vs. Hotel proximity to lifts. | Books lessons first, then lodging. Terrified of "Sold Out" dates. |
| **Booking** | **Package deals** (Lift + Lodging). Rental delivery services. | High cart value. Needs simplified itinerary management. |
| **The Day** | **Parking shuttle info**, Green/Blue trail grooming, bathroom locations. | Arrives late (getting kids ready). Needs frequent breaks/hot chocolate. |
| **Sharing** | **Family photos**, "Kids' first black diamond". | Facebook albums, family group chats. |

### Critical Data Points for OpenClaw
*   Ski School inventory/pricing.
*   "Groomed" status on Green/Blue runs.
*   Parking capacity/status.
*   On-mountain dining menus (kid-friendly options).

*   Event calendars (concerts, festivals).

## 4. The Park Rat ("The Content Creator")
**Archetype**: Young, Athletic, Digital-Native
**Motto**: *"Did you get the clip?"*

### Profile
*   **Demographics**: 15-25, Student/Young Adult.
*   **Skill Level**: High Freestyle skill, variable all-mountain skill.
*   **Gear**: Twin tips, baggy outerwear, helmet with audio, GoPro/Insta360.

### Predictive Needs (The Journey)
| Phase | Information Need | Predictable Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dreaming** | **Park setup photos**, Feature lists (rails, jumps, superpipe). | Follows park crew on IG. Chooses resort based on "Park builds". |
| **Planning** | **Park opening dates**, Competitions/Jams. | Travels in packs. Looks for cheap lift tickets or student passes. |
| **Booking** | **Cheapest access**. | Sleeps on floors, eats packed lunches. |
| **The Day** | **Park rope drop**, Lift lap speed. | Laps the same run (park lane) 20x a day. Hikes features. |
| **Sharing** | **Edits, Clips, Bails**. | TikTok/Reels heavy. Needs fast upload speeds. |

### Critical Data Points for OpenClaw
*   Detailed Terrain Park status (number of features, size).
*   Event schedules (rail jams).
*   Webcams focused on park.

## 5. The Never-Ever ("The First Timer")
**Archetype**: Anxious, Excited, Clueless
**Motto**: *"How do I stop?"*

### Profile
*   **Demographics**: Diverse, often invited by a group or trying a new hobby.
*   **Skill Level**: None.
*   **Gear**: Renting everything (jacket, pants, helmet, skis).

### Predictive Needs (The Journey)
| Phase | Information Need | Predictable Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dreaming** | **"Is it hard?", "What do I wear?"** | Intimidated. Looking for "Beginner Packages". |
| **Planning** | **Lesson details**, Gear rental logistics. | Needs hand-holding. Confused by industry jargon ("piste", "gondola"). |
| **Booking** | **All-inclusive learn-to-ski packages**. | High risk of churn if booking is complex. |
| **The Day** | **Where to go?**, "Magic Carpet" status. | High stress arrival. Needs clear wayfinding to rental shop -> lesson meeting point. |
| **Sharing** | **"I survived"**, Selfies with gear. | Achievement unlocking. |

### Critical Data Points for OpenClaw
*   "Beginner Zone" status.
*   Rental shop wait times.
*   Weather (rain/extreme cold is a dealbreaker).
*   Clothing requirements/guides.
